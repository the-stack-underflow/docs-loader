gcd(
    a: anytype, 
    b: anytype,
) @TypeOf(a, b)

Returns the greatest common divisor (GCD) of two unsigned integers (a and b) which are not both zero. For example, the GCD of 8 and 12 is 4, that is, gcd(8, 12) == 4.
