shiftRight(
    r: *Mutable, 
    a: Const, 
    shift: usize,

r = a >> shift r and a may alias.

Asserts there is enough memory to fit the result. The upper bound Limb count is a.limbs.len - (shift / (@sizeOf(Limb) * 8)).
