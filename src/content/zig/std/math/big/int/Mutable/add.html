add(
    r: *Mutable, 
    a: Const, 
    b: Const,
) void

r = a + b r, a and b may be aliases.

Asserts the result fits in r. An upper bound on the number of limbs needed by r is math.max(a.limbs.len, b.limbs.len) + 1.
