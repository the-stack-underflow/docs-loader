byteSwap(
    r: *Mutable, 
    a: Const, 
    signedness: Signedness, 
    byte_count: usize,

r = @byteSwap(a) with 2s-complement semantics. r and a may be aliases.

Asserts the result fits in r. Upper bound on the number of limbs needed by r is calcTwosCompLimbCount(8*byte_count).
