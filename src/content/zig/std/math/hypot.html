hypot(
    comptime T: type, 
    x: T, 
    y: T,
) T

Returns sqrt(x x + y y), avoiding unnecessary overflow and underflow.

Special Cases:

  • hypot(+-inf, y) = +inf
  • hypot(x, +-inf) = +inf
  • hypot(nan, y) = nan
  • hypot(x, nan) = nan
