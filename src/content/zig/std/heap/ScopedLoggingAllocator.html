ScopedLoggingAllocator(
    comptime scope: anytype, 
    comptime success_log_level: std.log.Level, 
    comptime failure_log_level: std.log.Level,
) type

This allocator is used in front of another allocator and logs to std.log with the given scope on every call to the allocator. For logging to a std.io.Writer see std.heap.LogToWriterAllocator
