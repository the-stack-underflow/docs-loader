runEmulatable(
    exe: *LibExeObjStep,
) *EmulatableRunStep

Creates an EmulatableRunStep with an executable built with addExecutable. Allows running foreign binaries through emulation platforms such as Qemu or Rosetta. When a binary cannot be ran through emulation or the option is disabled, a warning will be printed and the binary will NOT be ran.

Parameters

exe: *LibExeObjStep,
