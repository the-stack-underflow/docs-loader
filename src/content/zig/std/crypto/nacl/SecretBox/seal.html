seal(
    c: []u8, 
    m: []const u8, 
    npub: [nonce_length]u8, 
    k: [key_length]u8,
) void

Encrypt and authenticate m using a nonce npub and a key k. c must be exactly tag_length longer than m, as it will store both the ciphertext and the authentication tag.

c: []u8,
