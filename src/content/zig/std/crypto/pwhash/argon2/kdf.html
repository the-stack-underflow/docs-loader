kdf(
    allocator: mem.Allocator, 
    derived_key: []u8, 
    password: []const u8, 
    salt: []const u8, 
    params: Params, 
    mode: Mode,
) KdfError!void

Derives a key from the password, salt, and argon2 parameters.

Derived key has to be at least 4 bytes length.

Salt has to be at least 8 bytes length.
