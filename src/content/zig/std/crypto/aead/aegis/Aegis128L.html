AEGIS is a very fast authenticated encryption system built on top of the core AES function.

The 128L variant of AEGIS has a 128 bit key, a 128 bit nonce, and processes 256 bit message blocks. It was designed to fully exploit the parallelism and built-in AES support of recent Intel and ARM CPUs.
